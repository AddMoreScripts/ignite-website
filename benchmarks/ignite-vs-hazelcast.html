Ignite vs Hazelcast Benchmarks

Benchmarks were performed on Ignite 1.5.0-b1 and Hazelcast 3.6-EA2 to evaluate and compare their performance for various atomic and transactional cache operations, and SQL based cache queries. The results obtained provide throughput, latency, and percentile probes measuring various metrics during benchmark execution.

Deadlock Free Transactions (Optimistic, Serializable)


Hazelcast does not have support for deadlock-free transactions, so we are comparing Ignite deadlock-free transactions to Hazelcast pessimistic transactions.
